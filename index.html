APQ Guide

Amorian Challenge / GMS Reference

Entry Getting In ▼
Lip Lock Key
Entrance Ticket
  • 1Farm 10 Lip Lock Keys from Indigo Eyes and Crystal Boars near Amos' Training Ground (a few maps right of New Wedding Hall).
  • 2Turn them in to Amos the Strong to receive an Entrance Ticket. You can get a new ticket every 6 hours.
  • 3All 6 party members use their tickets to enter the pre-entry map. Once everyone is present, the leader talks to Amos to start.
⚠ Party of exactly 6 required. If anyone leaves or disconnects mid-run, the PQ cannot be completed. Both male and female characters are required, but an equal split is not.
Stage 1 Magik Mirror: The Fierry ▼
Amos
Glimmer Man
Magik Fierry B
Wing Hammer
Mirror Shard
  • 1The leader talks to Amos to spawn 3 portals. The correct portal is random, pick one until you land in the middle area of the map.
  • 2Someone else (not the leader) talks to Amos to shuffle the portals. The leader holds ↑ up so they enter the moment one activates.
  • 3Signs in the middle direct male characters right (Crystal Boars + Magik Fiarry As) and female characters left (Indigo Eyes + Magik Fiarry As). Kill all monsters on both sides.
  • 4The leader races to the bottom and talks to The Glimmer Man before mobs fully spawn. If fast enough, Magik Fierry B spawns immediately. Otherwise you wait for all mobs to die first.
  • 5The Fierry flies around the map, someone kills it to drop the Wing Hammer.
  • 6Whoever picks it up drops the Wing Hammer on the Magik Mirror above the center of the map to shatter it, producing a Magik Mirror Shard.
  • 7The leader delivers the Shard to Glimmer Man to clear. Everyone talks to Amos to proceed.
💡 Tip: The leader getting to Glimmer Man fast skips the mob-clear wait and spawns the Fierry immediately. This is the biggest time saver in Stage 1.
Stage 2 Heart Strings: The Sealed Room ▼
  • 1Three ropes hang from a Sun, Star, and Moon. The correct number of people on each rope must match the hidden combination, 5 people total across the three ropes.
  • 2Use the portals at the bottom to reach the ropes (press ↑, then jump and hold ↑ to cling). The stage layout changes based on server time (day / evening / night) but the mechanic is the same.
  • 3The leader gets 7 attempts and receives a clue after each: how many ropes have the correct count. If all 7 are used, monsters spawn and the combo resets.
  • 4Strategy: start all 5 on the left rope (5-0-0), then shift people one at a time, calling out the new distribution (e.g. "410", "320") until you find the right combo.
→ Use the Stage 2 tab for the interactive decision tree that walks you through every Same/Different result to the answer.
💡 Tip: Some paths have only one valid next step, the tool shows a "Next" button automatically for those.
Stage 3 Twisted Switcher: Platform Puzzle ▼
  • 19 platforms total: 4 top (A, 2, B, 3) and 5 bottom (C, 6, D, 9, 5). The correct combination uses 5 of these 9.
  • 2Start at A2B3C: 4 people on the top, 1 on the bottom. The leader clicks Amos to trigger a guess.
  • 3Each wrong guess spawns Crystal Boars (knock people off platforms, not present in GMS) and Slimes. Count the slimes. That number equals how many platforms are currently correct.
  • 4The bottom person moves one platform right after each count: C → 6 → D → 9 → 5. The leader calls out the running tally (e.g. "3", "32", "323"…).
  • 5Once the pattern is identified, move the party to the correct bottom platforms. Then brute-force the top using the KPQ method: try A2, AB, A3, 2B, 23, B3 until it clears.
→ Use the Stage 3 tab for the interactive slime counter. Type in counts as they're called out and it identifies the pattern automatically.
💡 Courtesy: Put a teleport class on the bottom since they move the most. Don't AFK.
⚠ Do NOT kill slimes until the full count is confirmed. Killing early miscounts and wastes an attempt.
Stage 4 Last Man Standing: Code Pieces ▼
Cupid Code Piece
  • 1Kill the mobs to drop Cupid Code Pieces. Need 50 total.
  • 2Takes roughly 1 minute for 50 to drop if all mobs are killed every spawn.
  • 3The leader needs to reach 50 pieces, so don't loot the pieces yourself. It just slows things down.
⚠ Don't loot the code pieces unless you're the leader. It doesn't break anything but it slows the leader down.
Stage 5 Fluttering Hearts: The Gates ▼
  • 1The entire party runs right across the map. You'll be swarmed by various mobs and lava columns that deal little damage and can mostly be ignored.
  • 2Not far behind is a swarm of Rombots dealing 10,000 damage per hit. Keep moving and don't fall behind.
  • 3While running, destroy the Gates with normal attacks. At the very end, jump onto the elevated platform to reach Amos.
  • 4Once the entire party arrives, talk to Amos to clear.
⚠ Use normal attacks on gates. Skills like Hurricane or high-damage AoE may not register on the gates. Switch to a basic attack if a gate isn't opening.
Stage 6 Love Hurts: Geist Balrog ▼
Geist Fang
Amos
  • 1Party starts top-left. Amos requires the full party present before beginning, so wait for everyone.
  • 2Fight Geist Balrog across 3 phases: Jr. Balrog, then Crimson Balrog, then Geist Balrog.
  • 3Pick up the Geist Fang that drops. The leader delivers it to Amos to clear the stage.
After Stage 6, depends on party composition:

Not 3♂/3♀ split: Sent directly to the normal Bonus Stage. Turn off pet auto-loot now.

3♂/3♀ split: Sent to the Couples Bonus Room. If all 3 pairs are in-game married couples, an extra challenge is available for a Legendary Elias Cape / Cecelia Cloak, but these are outdated and not worth it for most parties. If you're not 3 married couples, Amos sends you to the normal bonus stage anyway.
💡 Tip: Turn off pet auto-loot before Stage 6 ends so you're in control of what you pick up in the bonus stage.
Bonus Amos' Vault: Apple Frenzy ▼
Onyx Apple
Pine Chew
  • 1You have 60 seconds to hit as many boxes as possible with various skills.
  • 2Collect what drops. Onyx Apples are the main prize. Other drops include Crystalized Pineapple Chews, Cecelia's Earrings, Moonstar Earrings, Celestial Earrings, Amorian Rice Cookies, and Amorian Relaxers.
Crystalized Pineapple Chews grant a +20 attack buff lasting 7 minutes. Stackability TBC, grab them regardless.

Onyx Apples are the most valuable drop.
⚠ Turn off pet auto-loot and do not steal apples from other people. Only loot what drops near you. Apples are the whole point of the run for most people.
💡 Tip: Spread out and use AoE / multi-hit skills to break boxes quickly. 60 seconds goes fast.
